How to Login to Your Bellsouth Account?

Complete guide to accessing your Bellsouth email and account services. Learn how to troubleshoot common login issues and secure your account.

Bellsouth Account Login Guide

Bellsouth.net email accounts are now part of AT&T's email services. If you're trying to access your Bellsouth email login, you'll need to go through the AT&T login portal. This comprehensive guide will walk you through the Bellsouth login process, troubleshooting tips, and security recommendations.

Steps for Bellsouth Email Login

1
Open your web browser and navigate to the AT&T login page (currently hosted at att.com)
2
Enter your Bellsouth email address in the format username@bellsouth.net in the email field
3
Enter your password that you created for your Bellsouth email account
4
Click the "Sign In" button to access your Bellsouth email account
5
If prompted for additional verification, follow the on-screen instructions to complete two-factor authentication

Note: If you haven't logged into your Bellsouth account in a while, you may need to migrate your account to AT&T's system first. Look for the "Need to migrate your account?" link on the login page if you're having trouble.

Troubleshooting Bellsouth Login Issues

Common Bellsouth Login Problems and Solutions

I can't remember my Bellsouth email password

Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You'll need to verify your identity through the email or phone number associated with your account to reset your password.

My Bellsouth login says "invalid credentials"

Double-check that you're entering your full email address (including @bellsouth.net) and that your CAPS LOCK key isn't on. If you're certain the password is correct, try resetting it.

I'm being asked to migrate my Bellsouth account

Follow the migration process to transfer your Bellsouth email to AT&T's current system. This is a one-time process required for accounts that haven't been accessed in a while.

Security Alert: Never enter your Bellsouth login credentials on any website that isn't an official AT&T domain. Phishing scams often target Bellsouth email users.

Alternative Methods for Bellsouth Email Access

Accessing Bellsouth Email Through Email Clients

You can set up your Bellsouth email in desktop or mobile email clients like Outlook, Apple Mail, or Thunderbird using these IMAP settings:

  • Incoming Mail Server: imap.mail.att.net (Port 993 with SSL)
  • Outgoing Mail Server: smtp.mail.att.net (Port 465 with SSL)
  • Username: Your full Bellsouth email address
  • Password: Your Bellsouth email password

Mobile Access for Bellsouth Email

For smartphones and tablets, you can either:

  1. Use the AT&T Mail app (available for iOS and Android)
  2. Configure your device's native email app with the IMAP settings above
  3. Access through a mobile web browser at the AT&T login page

Security Tips for Your Bellsouth Account

Protecting Your Bellsouth Login

  • Create a strong, unique password that you don't use for any other accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication for additional security
  • Regularly check your account for suspicious activity
  • Keep your recovery email and phone number updated
  • Be cautious of phishing emails pretending to be from Bellsouth or AT&T

Password Tip: AT&T/Bellsouth passwords must be between 8-20 characters and include at least one letter and one number. Special characters are allowed but not required.

Bellsouth Account Management

After Successful Bellsouth Login

Once you've logged into your Bellsouth email account, you can:

  • Manage your email folders and filters
  • Update account security settings
  • Change your password or recovery options
  • Access AT&T services if you're also a customer
  • Set up email forwarding or auto-replies

Bellsouth Account Recovery Options

To ensure you can always access your account:

  1. Set up a recovery email address
  2. Add a mobile phone number for verification texts
  3. Keep your security questions updated
  4. Consider saving backup codes if available
